Overview

What is a Cyber Risk Assessment?

A cyber risk assessment is a process of identifying, analyzing, and evaluating potential cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities within an organization's IT infrastructure. It helps organizations understand their security posture and prioritize risk mitigation efforts using renwoned Security Conrols and formats.

This tool was created to help organisations and individuals who lack expertise in the Cyber Security field apply formal Frameworks such as the ISO 27001, NIST CSF/800-53, MITRE ATT&CK. The tool provides a likely risk score for each identified threat or vulnerability and provides advice on how to mitigate them.

Who is it for?

Designed for:

  • IT Security Professionals
  • Small businesses without dedicated security teams
  • Individuals interested in assessing their personal cybersecurity risks
How to Use Our Tool
    1. Identify your assets and data
    2. Choose the appropriate framework (e.g., ISO 27001, NIST CSF, MITRE ATT&CK)
    3. Select the appropriate risks and vulnerabilities to your assets
    4. Review the risk scores and mitigation recommendations
